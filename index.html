About Us

Urban Brew is a family-owned, community-focused coffee shop and cafe, notably featuring a 3,500 sq ft space with a supervised indoor play area for children, designed as a "you can sip with us" safe space for parents. They specialize in in-house roasted coffee, unique "croffles" (croissant-waffles), and fresh pastries. Key Aspects of Urban Brew: Locations & Concept: They have locations in Ottawa (130 Besserer St) and Whitby (405 Dundas St West). The concept focuses on combining a high-quality cafe experience with a dedicated, safe play area for children, open typically from 8 am for play. Coffee Quality: Urban Brew roasts their beans in-house daily, using single-origin beans with a grade of 88 or higher. They offer a rotating daily brew, espresso, and specialized "girly lattes". Menu & Amenities: In addition to coffee, they serve a variety of fresh-baked pastries and pies. The space is designed to be relaxing for parents while children engage in sensory and imaginative play. Hours & Pricing: The cafe generally opens around 7 am, with the play area opening at 8 am, operating until 4:00 pm (Monday-Friday) and 8 am-2 pm on weekends. Scheduled play sessions (30-90 minutes) are available for a fee, with free play often available on Mondays. Urban Brew Café serves handcrafted coffee made from premium beans. We focus on comfort, quality, and creating a relaxing space.
